Downloads

Install our browser extensions to get instant information about the Terms of Service and Privacy Policies of your favourite websites!

Please select your web browser: Mozilla Firefox - Google Chrome - Opera - Safari - Internet Explorer - Microsoft Edge.

Mozilla Firefox add-on

This add-on will add a small icon on the right side of your address bar. Click on the icon to get the summary of the terms, provided by our contributors on ToS;DR. When you land on a website with a low rating, a small notification will pop up in the bottom right hand corner to warn you.

If you are browsing a website that is not yet in our database, the icon will not appear.
